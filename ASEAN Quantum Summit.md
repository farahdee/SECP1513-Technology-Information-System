# ASEAN Quantum Summit

**Date:** 11 December 2025  
**Time:** 2:00pm - 5.00pm  
**Venue:** Dewan Sultan Iskandar, UTM

---

## Summary
The session introduced the Centre for Quantum Technologies (CQT) and its role as a national hub for advancing quantum research through collaboration with leading institutions such as NUS and partner universities. The talk highlighted six major research domains, including quantum computing, communication and security, sensing, devices, biology, and interdisciplinary quantum science.

It also outlined how quantum technologies are translated into real-world impact through application domains like computational biology, quantum chemistry, finance, and combinatorial optimisation. A layered platform architecture was presented, covering everything from low-level hardware control and error correction to high-level APIs and hybrid classical-quantum workflows.
